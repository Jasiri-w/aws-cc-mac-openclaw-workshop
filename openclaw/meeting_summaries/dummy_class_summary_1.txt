# **Course Summary: Introduction to Computational Systems (COMP 2101)**

## **Course Overview**
COMP 2101 introduces students to the foundational concepts of computational systems, including operating systems, cloud infrastructure, virtualization, and distributed computing. The course blends theory with hands‑on practice, giving students experience with Linux environments, cloud platforms, and containerized workflows.
