# **Course Summary: Business Strategy & Entrepreneurship (BUS 2015)**

## **Course Overview**
BUS 2015 equips students with strategic thinking frameworks and entrepreneurial skills to evaluate market opportunities, develop business plans, and lead ventures. The course combines case studies, guest lectures from industry leaders, and hands-on business development exercises.

## **Major Coursework & Deadlines**

### **1. Weekly Case Analysis**
- **Description:** Short written analysis of provided business case studies.  
- **Weight:** 20%  
- **Deadline:** Every Tuesday at 9:00 AM  

---

### **2. Business Plan Competition**
- **Description:** Teams develop a comprehensive business plan for a startup or new product launch.  
- **Weight:** 30%  
- **Milestones:**  
  - **Executive Summary:** **October 12**  
  - **Full Plan Submission:** **November 2**  
  - **Pitch Competition:** **November 15** (live presentation)  

---

### **3. Industry Guest Interview**
- **Description:** Students interview an entrepreneur or business leader and prepare reflective report.  
- **Weight:** 15%  
- **Due Date:** **October 31**  

---

### **4. Strategic Analysis Exam**
- **Description:** Comprehensive exam on market analysis, competitive positioning, and strategy frameworks.  
- **Weight:** 35%  
- **Date:** **December 10**  

---
