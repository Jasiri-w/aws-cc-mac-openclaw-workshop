# **Course Summary: Environmental Science & Sustainability (ENVS 2010)**

## **Course Overview**
ENVS 2010 examines the interaction between human societies and natural systems, focusing on climate science, resource management, and sustainable solutions. Students learn environmental principles, explore real-world case studies, and propose solutions to contemporary environmental challenges.

## **Major Coursework & Deadlines**

### **1. Weekly Reading Journals**
- **Description:** Reflective entries on assigned environmental readings and articles.  
- **Weight:** 15%  
- **Deadline:** Every Friday at 5:00 PM  

---

### **2. Field Study Report**
- **Description:** Students conduct observations and analysis during outdoor lab sessions; submit detailed field notes and interpretations.  
- **Weight:** 25%  
- **Submission Date:** **November 8**  

---

### **3. Sustainability Proposal Project**
- **Description:** Design a realistic sustainability solution for an identified environmental problem (local or global).  
- **Weight:** 35%  
- **Proposal Outline:** **October 15**  
- **Final Submission:** **December 1**  
- **Deliverables:**  
  - Feasibility analysis  
  - Implementation roadmap  
  - Budget estimation  
  - Presentation slides  

---

### **4. Final Exam**
- **Description:** Comprehensive exam covering climate systems, ecosystems, and sustainable development.  
- **Weight:** 25%  
- **Date:** **December 19**
